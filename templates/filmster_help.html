Goal: Guess the correct movie based on the hints provided. You have 3 hints and 3 tries, so think carefully before choosing!

How it works:

  1. You'll see 3 hidden hint boxes that corresponds to the movie.
  2. Tap the "Reveal Hint" button to uncover one hint at a time.
  3. After reading the hints, choose your answer from the 4 movie options below.
  4. If your answer is correct, the button will turn green.
  5. If your answer is wrong, it will turn red and you'll lose one try.
  6. You can still reveal more hints if you're unsure!
  7. The game ends when you guess the correct movie or run out of tries.
